About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival roguelike game where your skills and strategy determine how long you can withstand the relentless waves of monsters hunting you down.

As you survive longer, you'll gain experience to level up and choose from a variety of powerful abilities and weapons. But beware - the monsters become stronger and more numerous as time passes!

With procedurally generated maps and randomized power-ups, every playthrough offers a unique challenge that will test your adaptability and quick thinking.

Game Features

  • Multiple playable characters with unique abilities and playstyles
  • Dozens of weapons, passive items, and power-ups to discover and combine
  • Challenging boss encounters that will test your build's effectiveness
  • Progressive difficulty scaling that ensures a challenging endgame
  • Unlock new characters and abilities as you achieve gameplay milestones

How to Play

Getting Started

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Attacks are automatic and target nearby enemies
  • Collect gems to gain XP and level up
  • Choose new weapons or upgrades when you level up
  • Survive as long as possible!

Pro Tips

  • Focus on movement speed early to dodge enemies better
  • Build a balanced set of weapons that cover different attack patterns
  • Prioritize area damage weapons when facing large swarms
  • Don't forget defensive upgrades - they're crucial for survival!
  • Regeneration items become more valuable in the later game

Character Guide

  • Warrior - High HP, strong against single targets
  • Mage - Low HP, powerful area damage
  • Ranger - Balanced stats, high movement speed
  • Cleric - High regeneration, support abilities
